About Us

Flokki1 ehf. is a two-man specialist workshop based in Hveragerdi, Iceland. The founder is known locally for painting all of his own cars to a professional standard and has years of experience in car mechanics. His partner is an exceptionally skilled welder whose precision and craftsmanship speak for themselves.

Together we have the skills, the equipment, and the passion to handle any job — from full car resprays and bodywork restoration to structural welding and mechanical repairs. We work on classic marques like BMW and Jaguar, treating every car with the care it deserves.

What We Do

Painting, welding, bodywork, and mechanics — all under one roof

Body Restoration & Repair

We strip old paint, remove rust and corrosion, repair or replace damaged panels, and prepare the body with precision filler and sanding for a flawless foundation.

Professional Car Painting

Our dedicated paint booth with infrared drying lamps delivers durable, showroom-quality finishes. Full respray, primer, base coat, and clear coat — any colour you want.

Welding & Metal Fabrication

TIG and MIG/MAG welding for structural chassis repair, floor pan restoration, custom exhaust fabrication, and stainless steel work. Clean, strong joints every time.

Our Process

From wreck to perfection, step by step

1

Strip & Assess

We strip the car down, assess the damage, and plan the restoration work needed.

2

Weld & Repair

Rusted panels are cut out, new metal welded in, and structural integrity restored.

3

Prime & Prepare

The body is filled, sanded, and coated in primer — ready for the paint booth.

4

Paint & Finish

Professional spray painting and clear coat for a durable, factory-quality finish.
