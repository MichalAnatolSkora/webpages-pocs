Who we are

TWO MEN.
ONE MISSION.

Flokki1 ehf. is a specialist workshop based in Hveragerdi, Iceland. The founder is known locally for painting all of his own cars to a professional standard and has years of hands-on experience in car mechanics.

His partner is an exceptionally skilled welder whose precision speaks for itself. Together — with the right skills, top equipment, and a shared passion — we take on everything from full resprays to structural welding and mechanical repairs.

Signature service

Every car we paint gets the same treatment — meticulous prep, proper masking, and a dedicated booth with infrared drying. Primer, base coat, clear coat. Any colour. Showroom finish.
