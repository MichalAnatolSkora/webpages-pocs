# Company Profile

## About Us

We are a two-man specialist workshop dedicated to bringing old cars back to life. With hands-on expertise in automotive restoration, we handle every stage of the process — from stripping down rusty bodywork to delivering a showroom-quality finish.

## What We Do

### Car Body Restoration & Repair
We take on vehicles in any condition and restore their bodywork to factory standard or better. Our process includes:
- Stripping old paint and removing rust and corrosion
- Sheet metal repair and replacement of damaged panels
- Precision body filler and sanding to achieve smooth surfaces
- Full body priming and preparation for painting

### Professional Spray Painting
We operate a dedicated paint booth equipped with infrared drying lamps for a durable, flawless finish. Our painting services cover:
- Full respray in any colour
- Primer and base coat application
- Clear coat finishing
- Careful masking and protection of trim, glass, and interior components

### Welding & Metal Fabrication
Welding is at the heart of what we do. We work with both TIG and MIG/MAG techniques to deliver clean, strong joints on a variety of tasks:
- Structural chassis and underbody repair
- Floor pan and body panel welding
- Custom exhaust and intake pipe fabrication
- Stainless steel and mild steel work

### Classic & Vintage Car Specialists
We have experience working on a range of classic marques, including BMW and Jaguar. Whether it's a full ground-up restoration or targeted repair work, we treat every car with the care it deserves.

## Why Choose Us

- **Full in-house service** — bodywork, welding, and painting all under one roof
- **Skilled craftsmanship** — precise welds, smooth bodywork, and professional paint finishes
- **Passion for classics** — we understand old cars and the attention they require
- **Personal approach** — a small team means direct communication and dedication to every project
