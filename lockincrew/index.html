what we drop in for

/01 — strike team

zero-to-one product cells

you have a pitch, a deadline & a half-built figma. we turn up as a 3-person cell & ship a real v1 in 6 weeks. design, frontend, backend, infra, all in one room (or one discord).

6 wk window fixed price code is yours
/02 — rescue op

codebase rescues & rewrites

your repo is on fire, the original team is gone, & the next release is friday. we triage, we patch, we delete. we leave it boring & documented.

2-8 wk t&m handover doc
/03 — ai integration

llm features that actually ship

not another chatbot. real workflows: agents in your ops, eval harnesses, prompt & cache discipline, observability. we use claude as a tool, not a buzzword.

4 wk pilot opt-in retainer evals included
/04 — embedded operator

one of us, on your team, full lock-in

a single senior operator embeds in your team for one quarter. they don't attend standups; they ship. you keep the work, the context, & the muscle memory.

1 quarter monthly renewable
